MALAPPURAM

Malappuram is a district of the Kerala state with lots of point of interest for the tourist. The whole district contains the many of the places in which national parks, rivers, lake, waterfall, temples and lighthouses are the main. There are so many temples in this place to attract the people for showing the culture and tradition of Kerala. There is a Silent valley national park, which including backpacking, rivers, forest, tiger reserves and rainforest. This national park mostly covers the area of river and forest. Remaining part of the park is used to grow the tree plantation for fresh environment. In the other hand, there is a waterfall and a light house. This is only the place where you spend some time with your family or friends. . Here you see the coconut trees, all around the water. You can also invite for some of the water sports as well as the non water sports. In water sports you can use the boating, swimming, bamboo rafting and many more things. While in non-water sports you can play volleyball on the beach, sun bathing, playing with the sand, etc. After the enjoying whole day you need a place where you rest sometime then there are also some of the hotels and resorts in affordable price.
